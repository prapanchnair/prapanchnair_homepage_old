Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics (SPH)

\[ \rho \frac{D\mathbf{u}}{Dt} = \rho \mathbf{f} -\nabla p +\mu \nabla^2 \mathbf{u} \]
Approximation: \[ A(r)= \int_\Omega A(r') W(|r-r'|)dr' \]
Derivatives: \[ \nabla A(r)= \int_\Omega A(r') \nabla W(|r-r'|)dr' \]
  • Compressible & Incompressible (Weakly Compressible/ISPH)
  • Naturally modeled interfaces
