Ennai Kathirikai Kuzhambu

Ingredients (for 2 people)

  1. Brinjal - 4-5
  2. small onion - 1/2
  3. garlic -1/8 cup
  4. tomato -1/2 cup
  5. tamarind - 1/4 cup
  6. curry leave
  7. green chilly -1
  8. ginger small
  9. red chilli 11/2

Masala

  1. coriander 1.5
  2. turmer 1/4
  3. hing
  4. urud dal
  5. fenugreek seed
  6. jeera
  7. mustard
  8. red chilli 1
  9. salt
  10. oil
  11. water

Steps

  • heat oil in pan and fry brinjal for ten minutes
  • heat oil in another pan add mustard
  • add urud dal, fenugreek, jeera, red chilli
  • add chopped garlic and sautee for a min
  • add chopped onion, green chilli, ginger, and curry leaves
  • til onion brown
  • add choped tomatoes
  • red chili powder, coriander powder, hing, turmeric and salt
  • add tamarind juice
  • add little water and cook for 5 min
  • add the brinjal (fried for ten min ) to the gravy
  • cook for five min
